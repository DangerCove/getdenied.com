Block explicit songs

Automatically block songs that contain explicit lyrics, making sure kids (or other sensitive ears) don't get stirred up by Eminem's latest and greatest.

Note: filtering explicit contens requires an internet connection and that the track is available in Spotify's library. It will filter tracks on Spotify and iTunes though.

As seen on the internet

Customizable rules

Songs can be blocked by their artist, title or album, allowing you to truly enjoy Top 40 playlists, the Christmas season and Spotify Radio.

Make sure you never hear another live session or karaoke version with Denied.

Now Playing info

See which track is currently playing and easily create new rules by clicking on the title, artist or album.

Shows album art and track information from Spotify and iTunes!

Skip repeating tracks

Automatically skip tracks that you've played before in the past few hours.

Works with Spotify and iTunes

Whether you want to block songs while listening to Spotify Radio or your iTunes librabry,
Denied has you covered.

Stunning in Dark Mode

Apple's latest iteration of macOS introduces a brand new option that allows you to turn the entire UI dark. The fittingly called Dark Mode soothes your eyes in low light environments and feels raw and powerful.

From launch Denied is ready to support this new display mode. It even takes advantage of the customizable accent color. Try it!
