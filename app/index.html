Or any other band, song or album

Imagine listening to a great Spotify radio station or playlist, and all of a sudden that song or band you can't stand comes up. Don't you hate that? I sure do!

Denied is a Mac app that monitors Spotify and skips songs that match the filters that you've setup. Get Denied and enjoy the Christmas season, Top 40 playlists and Spotify radio without the annoying songs you don't like!

Almost Done

Denied is almost ready to go and will be available on the Mac App Store in the first half of September.

Super flexible filters

Skip songs based on its artist, title or the album is appeared on.

Cool notifications

Denied will optionally let you know when it's skipped a track. It will also keep track of how often a filter has been used.

Yea, statistics!

Easily switched off

In the mood for some terrible music? Just switch Denied off for a moment.

Requirements

  • Currently only works with Spotify. Support for more music apps and services is planned.
  • A 64bit Mac running OS X Mavericks (10.9) or Yosemite (10.10).
  • A distaste for certain bands, songs or albums.
