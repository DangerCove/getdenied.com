Denied for Mac sees what you're playing in Spotify, Rdio and iTunes and automatically skips songs that you dislike.

Tracks can be skipped by their artist, title, album, whether they contain explicit lyrics, or if you’ve heard them before in the past few hours.

As seen on the internet

Found footage

A 19 second clip showing how Denied saved someone's ears when a random unwanted track appeared in Spotify.

Works with Spotify, Rdio and iTunes

Whether you want to skip tracks while listening to Spotify Radio, Rdio Stations or your iTunes librabry, Denied has you covered.

Customizable rules

Tracks can be skipped by their artist, title or album, allowing you to truly enjoy Top 40 playlists, the Christmas season, Rdio Stations and Spotify Radio.

Make sure you never hear another live session or karaoke version with Denied.

Cool notifications

Receive notifications when a track is skipped (can be disabled) and keep track of how often a rule has been used.

Yeah, statistics !

Easily toggled

In the mood for some awful music? Just disable skipping for a moment.

Requirements

  • Works with the Spotify and Rdio desktop apps and iTunes.
  • Requires a Mac running OS X Yosemite (10.10).
  • A distaste for certain bands, songs or albums, explicit lyrics or repeating songs.
