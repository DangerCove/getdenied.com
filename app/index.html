Imagine listening to a great Spotify radio station or playlist, and all of a sudden that song or band you can't stand comes up. So annoying!

This handy Mac app monitors Spotify and skips songs that match the filters that you've setup. Get Denied and enjoy Top 40 playlists, the Christmas season and Spotify radio without the annoying songs you don't like.

Found footage

A 19 second clip showing how a brave music enthusiast saved his or her ears when a random unwanted track appeared.

Super flexible filters

Skip songs based on its artist, title or the album it appears on. You can even specify how keywords are matched: should the artist/title/album contain the keywords, start or end with them or match the keywords exactly.

Cool notifications

Receive notifications when a track is skipped (can be disabled) and keep track of how often a filter has been used.

Yeah, statistics !

Easily switched off

In the mood for some awful music? Just disable skipping for a moment.

Requirements

  • Currently only works with Spotify. Support for more music apps and services is planned.
  • A Mac running OS X Mavericks (10.9) or Yosemite (10.10).
  • A distaste for certain bands, songs or albums.
