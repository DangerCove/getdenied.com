Or any other band, song or album

Imagine listening to a great Spotify radio station or playlist, and all of a sudden that song or band you can't stand comes up. Don't you hate that? I sure do!

This handy Mac app monitors Spotify and skips songs that match the filters that you've setup. Get Denied and enjoy the Christmas season, Top 40 playlists and Spotify radio without the annoying songs you don't like!

Super flexible filters

Skip songs based on its artist, title or the album it appears on. You can even specify how keywords are matched: should the target contain the keywords, start or end with them or match the keywords exactly.

Just disable a filter to not skip songs that match it temporarily, or remove it completely.

Cool notifications

Optionally receive notifications when a track is skipped and keep track of how often a filter has been used.

Yeah, statistics!

Easily switched off

In the mood for some awful music? Just disable skipping for a moment.

Requirements

  • Currently only works with Spotify. Support for more music apps and services is planned.
  • A Mac running OS X Mavericks (10.9) or Yosemite (10.10).
  • A distaste for certain bands, songs or albums.
