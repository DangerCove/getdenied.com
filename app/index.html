Denied for Mac sees what you're playing in the Spotify desktop app and automatically skips songs that you dislike.

Tracks can be skipped by their artist, title or album, allowing you to truly enjoy Top 40 playlists, the Christmas season and Spotify Radio.

Super flexible rules

Skip songs based on its artist, title or the album it appears on.

You can even specify how keywords are matched: should the artist/title/album contain the keywords, start or end with them or match the keywords exactly.

  • Spotify desktop client. Support for more music apps and services is planned.
